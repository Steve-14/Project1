About the author of Whitebone


Steve Mole has been involved in Roleplaying Games for as long as he can remember.

Obsessed with collecting as many game systems as possible and writing a number of completely awful adventure scenarios to bore or terrorise his hapless fellow players.

Whether its "ADnD" or "Warhammer Fantasy Roleplaying", Steve has had a hand in playing or running the system.

Steve is currently running his players (victims) through a Pathfinder 2e game using the "Abomination Vaults" module. His next game will use the Vaessen rules, a newer game based around Nordic folklore with strong horror elements!


Steve lives in North Staffordshire with 2 neurotic dogs. He adores a Snickers but finds bananas tiresome.
